Background

I am Dr. Reuben Kamundi, a highly dedicated and skilled medical professional with a robust educational background. I hold a degree in Bachelor of Medicine, and Bachelor of Surgery from the University of Nairobi and A Master of Medicine In Plastic, Reconstructive, and Aesthetic Surgery

This advanced degree underscores my commitment to staying abreast of the latest advancements in medical science and surgical techniques. With over 8 years of experience as a medical doctor, I bring a wealth of practical knowledge and expertise to my practice.

As a practitioner specializing in plastic reconstructive and aesthetic surgery, I am poised to contribute my expertise to the medical community and make a positive impact in this dynamic field.
